About Us

‘My Neighbour Bakes’, a home-based bakery business which is primarily community focused. It is currently running it due to a personal passion for baking, but wants to expand to a real business.
The cakes are made from vegan ingredients due to the client’s preferences and to fill a gap in the market. Non-vegan ingredients can be used if specifically requested but are not preferred.
Business stands out due to the cake’s unique designs compared to other bakeries.
